Liuchao Jin Presents at 4DMDA 2025 in Singapore.

Liuchao Jin presented his latest research titled "Machine Learning-Driven Inverse Design of Hierarchical Architectures" at the 4th International Conference on 4D Materials Design and Additive Manufacturing (4DMDA 2025). Organized by A*STAR and the 4D Printing Society, the conference brought together global experts in smart materials, metamaterials, and additive manufacturing. His presentation contributed to advancing discussions on intelligent design, multi-material printing, and the future of programmable matter.

July 2025 4D Printing Society

Liuchao Jin to Join Prof. Chiara Daraio’s Group @ CalTech through ORAP in Summer 2025.

Liuchao Jin has been selected for the prestigious Overseas Research Attachment Programme (ORAP) of the Chinese University of Hong Kong. He will join Professor Chiara Daraio’s research group at the California Institute of Technology in Summer 2025. The ORAP initiative supports outstanding PhD students to gain international research experience and collaborate with world-renowned scholars in cutting-edge laboratories.

March 2025 Chinese University of Hong Kong

Liuchao Jin Honored with Outstanding Students Award of Chinese University of Hong Kong.

Liuchao Jin has been honored with the Outstanding Students Award of Chinese University of Hong Kong. This prestigious award recognizes exceptional academic achievements, research excellence, and significant contributions to the university community.

Feb 2025 Chinese University of Hong Kong

Liuchao Jin Invited to Global Young Scientists Summit 2025.

Liuchao Jin has been invited to represent Cornell University at the Global Young Scientists Summit (GYSS) 2025, an elite gathering of 340 young scientists nominated by 93 top institutions worldwide. Organized by the Singapore National Research Foundation (NRF), GYSS 2025 features 18 eminent scientists, including Nobel Laureates, Fields Medalists, and Turing Award winners.
